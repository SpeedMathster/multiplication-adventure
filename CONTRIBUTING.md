# Contributing to Multiplication Adventure

Thank you for your interest in helping improve **Multiplication Adventure**! 🎉

This project was created for my child to learn multiplication in a fun way, and I'm happy to share it with others. While this is a personal project, I welcome suggestions, bug reports, and ideas for improvement.

---

## 🐛 Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or something isn't working as expected:

1. Check if the issue has already been reported in the **Issues** tab.
2. If not, open a **new issue** and include:
   - A clear title and description.
   - Steps to reproduce the problem.
   - What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened.
   - Your browser and device (if possible).

---

## 💡 Suggesting Features

Have an idea for a new feature or improvement?

1. Open a **new issue** with the label `enhancement`.
2. Describe your idea clearly and explain why it would be useful.
3. If possible, suggest how it might work.

---
