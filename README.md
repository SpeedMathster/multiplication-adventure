# multiplication-adventure
A fun, free multiplication game for kids – quiz, speed drill, fill in the blank, and more.

## 🎮 How to Play

### 1. Play Quiz
- Choose the number of questions, time limit per question, and a specific table (or leave it on `0` for mixed).
- Click **Apply & Start**.
- Type your answer in the box and press **Enter** or click **Submit**.
- Your score is based on **accuracy (80%)** and **speed (20%)** – try to answer quickly and correctly!

### 2. Speed Drill
- Select a duration (1–30 minutes).
- Click **Start Drill**.
- Answer as many multiplication questions as you can before time runs out.
- Every correct answer adds to your score – wrong answers show the correct one.
- Your final result combines **accuracy (80%)** and **speed (20%)**.

### 3. Fill in the Blank
- Choose how many questions you want (5–100).
- Click **Start Session**.
- Fill in the missing number in each equation (e.g., `___ × 4 = 20` → answer is `5`).
- Keep going until you finish all questions – your accuracy and speed are recorded.

### 4. Flashcards
- Pick a specific table (1–12) or choose `0` for mixed practice.
- Click **New Card** to see a random equation.
- Try to solve it in your head, then click **Reveal Answer** to check.

### 5. Skip Counting
- Choose a table (from 2 up to your current max).
- See the full skip counting sequence automatically.
- Great for learning patterns and memorizing multiples.

### 6. Mastery
- Select any table (1 to your current max).
- See every multiplication fact for that table listed clearly.
- Use it as a quick reference or to practice reciting the whole table.

### 7. Pattern Explorer
- Click any pattern button (e.g., “9’s Finger Trick” or “Doubling Trick”).
- Read the explanation – these are mental math shortcuts that make multiplication easier.

### 8. Multiplication Chart
- Hover or click on any row, column, or cell.
- The chart highlights related products, helping you visualize patterns.
- Click **Reset Highlights** to clear the selection.

---

## ⚙️ Adjust the Game Range
At the top of the page, you can change the **table range** (e.g., from 1–12 to 1–20). This affects all tools – quiz, flashcards, skip counting, mastery, and the chart.

---

## 🧠 Tips for Parents & Teachers
- Use **Mastery** to introduce one table at a time.
- Challenge your child with the **Speed Drill** – start with 1 minute and increase as they improve.
- The **Fill in the Blank** tool builds algebraic thinking (finding the missing factor).
- All results are shown in a modal that can be **copied to clipboard** or shared.
